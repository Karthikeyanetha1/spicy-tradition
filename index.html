Our Story
A Family Recipe,
A Legacy of Taste
Founded in 2012, Spicy Tradition was born out of a grandmother's kitchen in Warangal. Today we bring those same recipes to Hyderabad — unchanged, uncompromised.

Happy Customers
2012 — The Beginning
Started as a small tiffin centre in Madhapur with just 8 tables and 5 dishes. Within 6 months, the word spread across HITEC City.
2016 — First Expansion
Opened our full restaurant with a proper dining hall, expanded menu, and our signature Gongura Mutton that became a neighbourhood obsession.
2020 — Going Online
Launched delivery on Swiggy and Zomato during the pandemic. Maintained a 4.8★ rating throughout with zero compromise on quality.
2024 — Today
Serving over 300 customers daily. Still using the same recipes from Warangal, still cooking with the same love we started with.
